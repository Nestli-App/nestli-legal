Cross-Border Data Transfer Notice

Last updated: 6 May 2026

This notice applies to all users globally, with specific guidance for users in mainland China who must understand cross-border data handling before registering, signing in, uploading data, or continuing to use Nestli.

Nestli is operated by an Australian entity. To provide account login, data sync, photo storage, family sharing, app security, error diagnostics, customer support, transactional emails, voice transcription, and AI-assisted features (where available), your personal information may be transferred to, stored in, accessed from, and processed in regions outside mainland China.

1. Overseas recipients

Overseas recipients that may receive or process your personal information include:

  • Nestli's Australian operating entity — operates the app, provides user support, handles data deletion and security requests.
  • Supabase — application database, authentication, and serverless functions.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — photo and file storage in the Asia Pacific (Sydney) region.
  • Amazon CloudFront — global content delivery network used to deliver photos and files to your device.
  • OpenAI — processes AI+ chat requests and voice transcriptions in supported regions.
  • Resend — sends transactional emails such as verification codes, password resets, email-change notifications, and family invites.

2. Purposes of processing

Cross-border processing serves the following purposes:

  • Creating, signing in, verifying, and securing user accounts.
  • Syncing and displaying baby profiles, care logs, photos, and family-shared data.
  • Providing family invitations, role management, and account deletion.
  • Sending verification codes, password resets, email-change notifications, family invites, and security alerts.
  • Diagnosing errors, maintaining application security, processing user requests, and improving service stability.
  • Providing voice transcription and AI-assisted features in supported regions.
  • Enforcing necessary regional identification and feature limitations.

3. Categories of personal information

Information that may be transferred and processed cross-border includes:

  • Account data: email, username, account identifier, login and verification info.
  • Device and usage data: device type, OS version, app version, basic regional signals, error logs, and feature usage.
  • Child-related information: nickname, date of birth, sex, growth, feeding, sleep, diaper, health, and milestone records.
  • Photos and user content: photos, captions, notes, and other content you actively upload.
  • Family sharing data: family relationships, invite records, member roles and permissions.
  • Voice data: audio submitted when using the voice-log feature; the audio is used only for transcription and is not persisted by Nestli.
  • AI request data: questions, necessary context, and any photos you choose to attach when using AI features in supported regions.

4. Storage locations

Text records, account data, and authentication services may be processed by Supabase in its regions. Photos are primarily stored in Amazon Web Services (AWS S3) Asia Pacific (Sydney) region and may be delivered through Amazon CloudFront's global edge network. Email, AI, transcription, backups, error diagnostics, and other technical services may take place in Australia, the United States, Singapore, or other regions outside mainland China.

5. Specific notice for users in mainland China

If you are located in mainland China, before registering, signing in, uploading data, or continuing to use the app you should separately confirm and consent that your account information, device information, usage records, family data, child-related information, photos, log records, and content you actively submit may be transferred to, stored in, accessed from, and processed in regions outside mainland China.

If you do not consent to the cross-border transfer and processing described above, please do not register, sign in, upload data, or continue to use this app.

6. User rights

You may exercise the following rights through in-app features or by contacting us:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Delete baby profiles, care logs, photos, or your account.
  • Withdraw consent or request account deactivation.
  • Request explanation of personal information processing rules and cross-border transfer arrangements.
  • Consult, complain, or request our assistance with data export and deletion.

Questions about this notice or cross-border data handling can be sent to:
